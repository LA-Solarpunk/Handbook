## Who Are We

We are a group of people who believe that technology can be empowered towards greater good instead of being used to extract and exploit resources, land, and people.

The group is organized around the Solarpunk movement of which there are many definitions and ideas. There is a manifesto which can be found [here](https://re-des.org/a-solarpunk-manifesto/)

## Mission Statement

LA Solarpunk is a maker collective that builds and explores sustainable and resilient technology. We do so in direct partnership with community and mutual aid organizations and as a collective exploring our own curiosity. We believe technology should serve people, not profit, so we aim to develop accessible, low-cost solutions to real needs, share every design and process openly, and create a community space at CRASH where anyone can learn, build, contribute, and connect. We aren't trying to make perfect solutions, but to iteratively push in the direction we believe in. We're working toward a future powered by community resilience, ecological responsibility, and tech that's actually in reach of the people who need it.
